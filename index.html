Jeemaa Intelligent Platform

System Status: Online // Youyi Cup Entry

The Jeemaa Intelligent Area and Country Studies Platform is an integrated intelligent platform designed to support teaching, research, and talent cultivation through advanced technological solutions.

01 Intelligent Collection

Global Monitoring Grid

The platform supports user-defined keyword configuration and utilizes distributed web crawling technologies together with authoritative data interfaces. It enables real-time monitoring and early warning of public opinion across diverse sources, providing comprehensive data coverage for research purposes.

02 Deep Processing

NLP Core Analysis

By applying natural language processing (NLP) and large language model technologies, the platform provides multilingual translation, sentiment analysis, opinion extraction, key point summarization, and bias detection. These capabilities enable researchers to derive meaningful insights from complex data sets.

03 Process Management

Workflow Protocol

The platform offers full-process online management of research activities, covering data collection, note organization, tool usage, and report writing. Research processes are traceable throughout their lifecycle, ensuring transparency and reproducibility in academic work.

04 Capability Cultivation

Simulation Training

Through task-oriented methodological guidance, simulation-based training, and continuous process feedback, the platform supports users in learning and applying research methods in real research scenarios. This approach fosters the development of critical research skills and methodological proficiency.

05 Co-Pilot Output

Human-Machine Link

Based on selected research topics, the platform can assist in generating report outlines and drafts. Researchers retain full control over content editing, enabling effective human–machine collaboration that enhances productivity while maintaining academic integrity.

System Summary

The Jeemaa Intelligent Platform integrates intelligent collection, deep processing, and process management to empower users. By combining advanced technologies with user-centric design, it creates a comprehensive ecosystem that supports all phases of academic research and talent development.
