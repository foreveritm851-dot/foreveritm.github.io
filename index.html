The Jeemaa Intelligent Area and Country Studies Platform is an integrated intelligent platform designed to support teaching, research, and talent cultivation in area and country studies. It brings together teaching management, knowledge learning, research training, and academic output within a unified digital environment, enabling more efficient and structured teaching and research practices.

Intelligent Information Collection

Comprehensive Monitoring of Global Information Sources

The platform supports user-defined keyword configuration and utilizes distributed web crawling technologies together with authoritative data interfaces. It enables real-time monitoring and early warning of public opinion across diverse sources, including global think tanks, mainstream media, and academic journals. This helps users systematically track regional developments and emerging trends.

Deep Information Processing

From Information Access to Actionable Insights

By applying natural language processing (NLP) and large language model technologies, the platform provides multilingual translation, sentiment analysis, opinion extraction, key point summarization, and bias detection. These functions significantly improve the efficiency and depth of information analysis and interpretation.

Research Process Management

End-to-End, Traceable Research Workflows

The platform offers full-process online management of research activities, covering data collection, note organization, tool usage, and report writing. Research processes are traceable throughout their lifecycle, supporting collaborative work and enabling the long-term accumulation of structured research knowledge assets.
